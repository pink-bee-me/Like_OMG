"The devil is in the details..."

Within this age old idiom lies a simple truth; the most detrimental mistake made while attempting any endeavor, is failure to give proper attention to the small details that make up each aspect of that endeavor.

Attention to detail (or lack there of) directly impacts the level of success any endeavor can possibly hope to acheive. This correlation between the level of success acheivable and the amount of attention paid to the details is applicable to all endeavors, no matter how big or small. The probablitiy of success and the quality of the attention paid to details are, in fact, directly proportionate. In short, the only thing that differentiates the "Great" from the "Good" and the "Good" from the "Bad" is the degree of completeness that is applied to the careful consideration of the details and the possible implications, the various affects, and the scope of these implications, and the way these things factor in and influence the outcome of the project. Failing to pay attention to detail is like purposely choosing to be mediocre at best. Where you had expected to produce an outcome that brought you great pride, instead you find yourself sitting there, dazed and confused, shaking your head, realizing that in your rush you made the choice to be less than stellar, ... or even worse, possibly offending the very audience you were trying to impress! On the other hand,... by paying attention to the details, making a point to give every aspect of your project careful consideration, it gives you the ability to tranform what may seem like an average idea at first glance, into something that is MAGNIFICENT!!! The take away here is this: Whatever one does should be done thoroughly; details are important. "God is in the detail", the original version of the aforementioned idiom, means that attention paid to small things brings forth big rewards... with this in mind, I believe it is fair to say that when you take the time to pay attention to the details you literally raise the bar with regard to the level of success that is possible to acheive , while also greatly increasing the probablity of taking that possibility and making it your reality!!!
